Privacy Policy

Helping Teachers provides simple, free classroom tools. Our tools are designed to work without user accounts, logins, or student registration.

We do not ask teachers or students to create accounts to use the tools. Any information typed into classroom tools, such as names or activity lists, stays in the browser and is not intentionally collected by Helping Teachers.

Some tools may use your browser storage to remember basic preferences or saved items on your device. You can clear this data from your browser settings at any time.

We may use basic analytics in the future to understand general site usage and improve the tools. We do not sell personal information.
